How To Buy Airtme From Your Bitcoin/Naira wallet On The Patricia App

How to buy Airtime from your btc or naira wallet

Follow the steps below

  • Sign in to your patricia app
  • On your dash board select the refill option
  • Select airtime to cash
  • Input the Phone number, choose the network, input the amount you choose to purchase and click on “Continue”.
  • Select payment mode via your btc or naira wallet
  • Easy.. Your transaction is completed.
