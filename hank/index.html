    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I connect my hank to my phone?

    • Login to your Patricia app.
    • Click on Hank
    • Click Connect Hank. N/B: Ensure your location service and Bluetooth is on.
    • Hold your hank close to your phone. Ensure it is less than 4 inches away.
    • Hold the button on your hank for a few seconds until you hear a beep and the LED light starts flashing.
    • Tap Continue. Keep your hank within 4cm proximity to your phone till the connection is complete. Your hank will be paired to your device.
    • Name your Hank

    Do I pay for a subscription on every individual hank I purchase?

    Hank subscription is account-based and not device-based.
    That means that you do not pay for a subscription for every unit of hank you purchase.
    Once you are subscribed to a plan on your account, you can add multiple hanks to it at a time.
    However, the number of hanks that can work on a particular subscription at a time depends on the subscription plan

    How do I find my hank using the app?

    • Open your app
    • Open Hank; you will see a list of all your devices connected to your account.
    • Click on the device you need to find.
    • Click on Hank it.

    Your hank would begin to ring.

    Can one hank be connected to different phones at the same time?

    No. You cannot connect one hank to different phones at the same time.

    Does Hank require an active internet to function?

    Your hank does not need an active internet to work.
    However, you would need the internet to access the hank application on your Patricia app
    if you want to ring your hank from your phone device.

    What if my phone is on Silent or DND?

    You hank override your phone setting and ring out even if your phone is on silent or on “Do not disturb” mode.
