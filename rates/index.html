Common questions about our rates

How frequently do rates change?

Rates are set based on market prices and fluctuations in the market can affect rates too. However, when you are trading with Patricia, you can be confident that you will be getting the best value (and rates) for your transactions.

Do rates affect my transactions with Patricia?

The rates given at the time a transaction was made remains the same irrespective of market fluctuations. (provided that the details provided at the time of the transaction was made are correct)

What does Patricia charge as withdrawal fees?

Patricia charges a withdrawal fee of N100 for all withdrawals from your wallet.

Are there any other fees?

Yes, there are other fees depending what you are doing or the type of transaction.

What are the withdrawal limitations?

The withdrawal limitations are based on the KYC level your account is on.

  • Default: Email verification
  • One time limit: NGN 1000
  • Daily Limit: NGN 1000

  • KYC Level 1: BVN verification
  • One time limit: NGN 500,000
  • Daily Limit: NGN 2,000,000

  • KYC Level 2: Government issued ID and Proof of Life Selfie
  • One time limit: NGN 2,000,000
  • Daily Limit: NGN 5,000,000

  • KYC Level 3: Utility Bill, Bank Statement and Indemnity Agreement
  • One time limit: NGN 5,000,000
  • Daily Limit: NGN 50,000,000
